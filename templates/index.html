The Complaint and Registration Management System is designed to streamline the process of submitting, tracking, and resolving complaints, as well as managing registrations for various services. Whether you're a user reporting an issue or an administrator handling requests, our platform ensures a seamless and efficient experience.

How to Use the Platform

Step 1: Register

Create an account by providing your details in the registration form. Verify your email to activate your account.

Step 2: Submit a Complaint

Log in and navigate to the complaint section to submit your issue. Provide details and attach any relevant documents.

Step 3: Track and Resolve

Monitor the status of your complaints or registrations in your dashboard. Receive updates and communicate with administrators.
